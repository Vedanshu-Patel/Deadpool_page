It's the Leader of the X Force, Deadpool

X-Force is a team of superheroes most commonly in association with the X-Men. The team first appeared in New Mutants #100 (April 1991) and soon afterwards was featured in its own series called X-Force. The group was originally a revamped version of the 1980s team, the New Mutants. X-Force's first leader was the mutant Cable. An offshoot of the X-Men, X-Force takes a more militant and aggressive approach towards its enemies compared to the X-Men. An alternate incarnation of X-Force appears in the 2018 , Deadpool as part of the X-Men series. A planned X-Force adaption by 20th Century Fox was in production, but it was stopped after Disney killed 20th Century Fox.
